LumenPnP - Pays For Itself

This calculator determines how many boards it takes to break even on a LumenPnP purchase, and compares the boards produced per hour against hand-population. It makes a few assumptions:

  • It takes approximately two hours to set up a LumenPnP for a job.
  • It takes about one minute to unload and load a board in the LumenPnP.
  • The LumenPnP is running at 1000 CPH
